About Kid Harvest

Where Every Kid Is a
Member of the Club!

Every service at Kid Harvest Clubhouse, we roll out the red carpet for your family! From the moment your kids walk through our doors, they'll step into a fun-filled, safe, and welcoming environment where they can learn about Jesus, build friendships, and grow in faith through exciting, age-appropriate experiences.

While your kids are laughing, learning, and making memories, you can relax knowing they are loved, cared for, and safe every step of the way. Whether it's your first visit or you've been part of the family for years — every child is welcomed and celebrated here.
