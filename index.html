About Kid Harvest

Where Every Kid Is a
Member of the Club!

Every service at Kid Harvest Clubhouse, we roll out the red carpet for your family! From the moment your kids walk through our doors, they'll step into a fun-filled, safe, and welcoming environment where they can learn about Jesus, build friendships, and grow in faith through exciting, age-appropriate experiences.

While your kids are laughing, learning, and making memories, you can relax knowing they are loved, cared for, and safe every step of the way. Whether it's your first visit or you've been part of the family for years - every child is celebrated here.

For Parents

Parents Matter
Here Too

We believe ministry works best when churches and families partner together. Our goal is to walk alongside parents as they raise children who know and love God — providing safe environments, trusted leadership, and meaningful experiences your whole family can grow through.

Background-Checked Leaders

Every volunteer and staff member is thoroughly screened and trained to care for your child with excellence.

Secure Check-In & Check-Out

Family Partnership
